Tên món ăn: Tangy cabbage slaw
Category: Vegetarian
Nguồn Gốc: Ukrainian

Nguyên liệu:
Mayonnaise: 250ml
Lemon: Zest and juice of 1
Cider Vinegar: 2 tablespoons
Wholegrain Mustard: 2 tablespoons
Celery Salt: 1 tsp
White Cabbage: 1/4 
Red Cabbage: 1/4 
Carrots: 2
Onion: 1 Diced
Celery: 2 sliced

Công thức:
step 1
Whisk together mayonnaise, lemon zest and juice, vinegar, mustard and celery salt in a small bowl, then season generously.

step 2
Add cabbage, carrots, onion and celery. Mix well and refrigerate. Can be made up to 1 day ahead.
