Tên món ăn: Vegan Chocolate Cake
Category: Vegan
Nguồn Gốc: American

Nguyên liệu:
Self-raising Flour: 1 1/4 cup
coco sugar: 1/2 cup
cacao: 1/3 cup raw
baking powder: 1 tsp
flax eggs: 2
almond milk: 1/2 cup
vanilla: 1 tsp
water: 1/2 cup boiling

Công thức:
Simply mix all dry ingredients with wet ingredients and blend altogether. Bake for 45 min on 180 degrees. Decorate with some melted vegan chocolate
